About Me

This is what I do.

As a senior at Temple University, I am embarking on an exciting journey into the world of Geographic Information Systems (GIS) technology. My passion for exploring the intersection of geography and technology has led me to dive into the intricacies of Arc GIS Pro, where I am honing my skills in spatial analysis, mapping, and data visualization. The dynamic and ever-evolving field of GIS captivates me, and I am thrilled to be at the forefront of utilizing cutting-edge tools to solve real-world problems.

In addition to my GIS endeavors, I am delving into the realm of coding through platforms like CodePen.io. This hands-on experience allows me to merge my interests in GIS with the power of programming, creating a unique skill set that positions me as a versatile and innovative professional. I am eagerly embracing the challenges and opportunities that arise as I bridge the gap between geographic information systems and the world of coding.

As I approach the culmination of my college journey, I am not only acquiring technical expertise but also cultivating a mindset that values creativity, problem-solving, and adaptability. With a foundation in GIS and coding, I am poised to make meaningful contributions in the ever-expanding field of geospatial technology, and I am excited about the possibilities that lie ahead in my professional journey.
